Privacy Policy

How ReliaDB collects, uses, and protects your information.

What Data We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Company name (optional)
  • Your message and any details you provide about your database setup

We do not collect any data beyond what you voluntarily provide through the contact form.

How We Use Your Data

The information you submit is used solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule assessment calls, and communicate about potential or ongoing engagements. We do not use your data for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt in.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies required for basic site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies.

Analytics

We do not currently run any analytics services on this website. No user behaviour is tracked or recorded.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and forwarded to us via email. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Netlify — hosts this website. Netlify may collect server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) as part of standard web hosting. See Netlify's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — serves the Inter typeface used on this site. Font requests may transmit your IP address to Google. See Google's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and manage any resulting business relationship. If you request deletion, we will remove your data promptly.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction — request that we limit the processing of your data.
  • Objection — object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@reliadb.com. We will respond within 30 days.

MySQL EXPLAIN Analyzer Tool

The MySQL and MariaDB EXPLAIN Analyzer runs entirely in your browser. No query plans, SQL statements, or schema data are transmitted to any server. All analysis is performed client-side using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your browser.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or to make a data request, contact us at support@reliadb.com.
